===== Job Description =====
Do you want to rethink how building projects are planned – not from the sidelines, but right at the heart of it?

At vyzn, we’re shaping the future of construction planning with a data- and AI-driven SaaS platform. In just a few clicks, developers, planners, and architects receive precise insights on emissions, energy, comfort, and costs - based on automatically refined 3D building models - along with AI-powered recommendations to support well-informed decisions.

As a Lead Developer (80–100%), you’ll be part of a team that doesn’t just think big, but also makes things happen. If you’re motivated to help shape an ambitious ETH spin-off that combines sustainability and AI, then you’re exactly who we’re looking for.

Tasks
You’ll take ownership of our technical foundation and guide a small but highly skilled development team (3–6 people). You’ll combine hands-on full-stack coding with technical leadership, ensuring that our platform remains scalable, robust, and future-ready as we expand into new markets.


Lead the technical development of our SaaS platform - from concept to deployment

Shape and evolve the software architecture to ensure scalability, performance, and maintainability

Mentor the development team, fostering clean code, knowledge sharing, and best practices

Collaborate closely with product and design to translate user needs into technical solutions

Coordinate technical priorities with the CTO and founders

Oversee code reviews, testing, and deployment pipelines


Requirements

5+ years of experience in software development, ideally in SaaS, data, or AI-driven platforms

Strong skills in TypeScript/Angular, Node.js, and cloud-based architectures (Azure or AWS)

Proven experience in leading small engineering teams or mentoring developers

A structured, pragmatic mindset — you know when to build fast and when to build for the long term

Excellent communication skills in English; German is a plus

Preferably based in Switzerland or Germany (hybrid or remote with occasional workshops), or in Egypt, where part of our engineering team works (remote-friendly, on-site workshops with the Egypt team).


Bonus


Interest in architecture, BIM, and sustainability in the built environment

Several members of our tech team are based in Egypt. Experience collaborating with teams across the MENA region, or familiarity with the culture is a plus


Benefits

Flexibility: Hybrid and remote work options to fit your lifestyle.

Growth: Learn every day and develop both professionally and personally through challenging development tasks.

A-Player Team: Collaborate with passionate doers who drive impact and innovation forward.

Impact: Contribute to a more sustainable, livable future with solutions that benefit both people and the planet.

Direct Collaboration: Work closely with the founding team and actively shape key projects.

Innovation at the Intersection of AI and Construction: Be part of groundbreaking platform innovation that combines cutting-edge technology with sustainable building solutions.

Long-Term Potential: Join a dynamic, growing startup with flat hierarchies, high ownership, and the opportunity for long-term collaboration.
