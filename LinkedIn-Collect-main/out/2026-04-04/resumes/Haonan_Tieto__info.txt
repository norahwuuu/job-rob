Job Description

Do you enjoy building end-to-end digital solutions and shaping them together with customers? At Tieto Tech Consulting, you will work across the full technology stack, influence technical decisions, and help transform business ideas into scalable, cloud-ready solutions. Do you enjoy working end-to-end on modern IT solutions and collaborating closely with customers? Then this role is for you!

We are looking for a (Senior) Fullstack Developer who wants to be part of Tieto Tech Consulting. Tieto Tech Consulting is the leading digital accelerator for innovation and sustainable value creation. We combine business design with software engineering to bring digital business to life.

With a global team of 9,000 colleagues, we are constantly striving to develop and grow. Our customers trust in our solutions, which is why we are continuously looking for new talents. We are continuing to strengthen our presence in the German market and are expanding our operations with a clear focus on future growth. To support this journey, we are looking for new colleagues to join our team in Berlin.

As a (Senior) Fullstack Developer, you will take ownership of technical solutions from concept to production, actively contributing to architecture, technology choices, and implementation quality. You will work closely with customers and interdisciplinary teams, contribute to technical decision-making, and ensure high-quality delivery across both frontend and backend.

When joining Tieto Tech Consulting as a (Senior) Fullstack Developer, you
Design, develop, and implement high-quality fullstack solutions for customer projects
Work directly with customers to understand business requirements and translate them into technical solutions
Develop scalable backend services and modern frontend applications
Ensure high standards for code quality, security, performance, and maintainability
Participate in customer workshops, agile ceremonies, and solution discussions
Stay up-to-date with emerging technologies and industry trends
Contribute to the design and implementation of cloud-native web solutions on hyperscaler platforms (e.g. Azure), including architecture decisions and best practices
Continuously evaluate and introduce new tools, frameworks, and practices
Who We Are Looking For
4+ years of experience as Fullstack Developer
Strong proficiency both in frontend (e.g. React and/or Angular) and backend technologies (e.g. Java and/or C#)
Experience with RESTful APIs and microservices as well as with databases and cloud services
Strong communication skills and a customer-oriented mindset
A curious mindset and willingness to learn and share knowledge
Leverage AI-assisted development tools (e.g. GitHub Copilot) to improve code quality, productivity, documentation, and problem-solving
Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines and cloud-based deployment workflows
Critically evaluate and responsibly apply AI tools in day-to-day development workflows
Very good German and English skills
As a person, we believe you are a team player who takes action and demonstrates responsibility to organize your work and solve your tasks. With our agile end-to-end development process, you will continue to both learn from, and coach your colleagues, so communication is important for us. Technology is developing fast, so learning new innovative technologies is something you strive to do.
