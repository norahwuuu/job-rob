岗位描述 (JD)
============================================================

At Formbricks, you have the chance to build the next generation of Experience Management software, a market with billion-dollar potential. As a Senior Frontend Engineer, you'll contribute to building popular open-source surveying infrastructure and shape the future of software that enhances user experience. We foster a culture of open feedback and view mistakes as learning opportunities, encouraging team members to take on challenging tasks and providing guidance for success.

Tasks

Lead the frontend development of the Formbricks XM Suite
Work together with a small but growing team of talented engineers
Tackle complex engineering challenges
Research, document and communicate architectural decisions
Get your hands dirty in the code, making sure everything is clean, scalable, and built to last. You'll set the bar for your team's tech decisions.
Be a central point of contact for others to drive collaboration and unblock when needed
Ensure the team follows best practices (e.g. DRY) while pushing the product forward
Hop on 1:1 meetings to tackle individual topics and bottlenecks
Team

We are a small remote-first team located across the globe. As a startup we execute fast and ship new production-ready features week by week. We maintain a culture of open, direct and respectful feedback. We understand mistakes as the quickest way to learn and appreciate that everyone makes them — including the founding team. We encourage our team members to pick up tasks that challenge them and enjoy providing guidance to be successful.

Application process

Written application
Interview call with CTO & CTO
One-day work-trial

At least 5 years of experience in software development in a product / SaaS environment.

Ability to articulate technical concepts clearly and keep your team and stakeholders informed with consistent, proactive updates.
Excitement about how AI-based coding tools and agents increase productivity and speed up shipping.
Passion for great design and creating fast, seamless user experiences, with a focus on browser performance and page speed optimization.
Knowledge of design patterns, applying them when they make sense to deliver practical, solution-oriented outcomes.
Ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment, balancing velocity with high-quality outcomes.
Ambitious and driven to push boundaries while maintaining an eye for detail and delivering results.

Strong collaboration skills — mentoring, sharing knowledge, and empowering your team to grow and succeed while building strong relationships within and across teams.
