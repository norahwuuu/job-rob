===== Job Description =====
Build the systems behind an AI agent that plans and executes marketing across real business platforms.
About Us:
We are building an AI platform that integrates directly with business systems and powers intelligent automation.
This is not just another SaaS backend. You’ll work on systems that process structured business data and drive AI-based workflows at scale.
Manta is an end-to-end trustable, Cloudflare deployed AI CMO agent that uses a per-user agent system approach to make hyper-personalized Action Plans for marketing companies, which manages the ad accounts for our customers. Using an in-house Context Graph-backed system you will build APIs and visualizations for cross-platform workflow visualizations and monitoring, dynamic validations, and user-controllable agentic execution.
On top of the above, you will take part in making sure the underlying Knowledge Graph is kept up to date in real-time with the latest data, with a fleet of Cloudflare Durable Objects consuming streams of data from a variety of integrations.
What You Will Do
Build and extend our agents using the Cloudflare Agents SDK and Durable Objects
Develop visualizations of massive action plans, with data and integrations spanning Meta, Google, Shopify, WooCommerce, and more.
Contribute to schema-driven integration tooling built from our internal data models
Work on edge computing services that process and stream structured datasets
Help scale per-tenant data environments powering AI decision logic using Knowledge/Context Graphs
Design and maintain scalable APIs and full-stack systems in TypeScript
Monitor user journeys using Posthog and take part in customer interviews to drive the next steps
