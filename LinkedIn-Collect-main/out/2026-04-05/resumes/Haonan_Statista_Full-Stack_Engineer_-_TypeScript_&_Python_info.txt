===== Job Description =====
At Statista, we’re all about facts and data, for we are the world's leading business data platform. By providing reliable and easy-to-use data as well as various data analytics products and services, we empower people worldwide to make fact-based decisions.

Founded in Hamburg in 2007, we have quickly grown into a global company with offices in major cities such as London, New York, Berlin and Tokyo. And we still have a lot of plans. Our constant growth does not only prove our success, but also keeps creating new development and career opportunities for our employees.

We value and celebrate our diverse culture. You are welcome here for who you are, no matter where you come from, what you look like, or whether you prefer bar graphs to pie charts. Your story matters – keep writing it as part of our team.

Are you ready to join us?

We are a fast-growing data-as-a-service company turning complex data into meaningful products that help businesses make smarter decisions. Our platform powers real-world applications across customer-facing interfaces and internal tools — and we're building the future of intelligent, data-driven software. We are now looking for a passionate Full-Stack Engineer to join us and help shape what comes next.

Your Role

As a Full-Stack Engineer, you will be at the heart of turning raw data into elegant, reliable products. You'll work across the entire stack — from crafting intuitive UIs to building robust backend services — and you'll own your work end to end. This is a role for someone who thinks beyond tickets, cares deeply about quality, and gets excited about the intersection of data and product.

Write well-structured, maintainable code in TypeScript and Python that your teammates will actually enjoy working with
Transform data into meaningful, impactful products our customers rely on every day
Build and evolve both customer-facing and internal UIs and services
Own the full software lifecycle: from design and development through code reviews, deployment, and long-term maintainability
Communicate fluently in English (written and spoken) — German is a welcome bonus
Your profile
5+ years of professional experience building front- and back-end services with a focus on seamless user experience
Strong proficiency in HTML5, CSS/SCSS, JavaScript/TypeScript, and modern frontend frameworks — especially React
Solid understanding of responsive web design and cross-browser compatibility
Proven ability to ship production-ready Python backends
A creative problem-solving mindset — you don't just fix bugs, you ask why they exist and you fix them
Genuine curiosity and openness to learning new technologies
A true team player with a strong sense of personal ownership
